The Blackfish - Orcinus Orca

Orcas are the largest extant members of the dolphin family. Males typically range from 6 to 8 meters (20 to 26 ft) long and weigh in excess of 6 tons. Females are smaller, generally ranging from 5 to 7 meters (16 to 23 ft) and weighing about 3 to 4 tons. The skeleton of the orca is typical for an oceanic dolphin, but more robust.
