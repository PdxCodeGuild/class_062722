Summer Camp 2022

Ages 5 & up

We are excited to offer our SUMMER CAMP for ages 5 & up. Each week will feature a new theme and will include gymnastics and ninja activities in our newly updated recreational area and our new Ninja Warrior Rig. crafts, games, experiments, movies, outdoor activities including water play, and more! Choose mornings, afternoons, or both!

*PLEASE NOTE: Snacks options will be available; however, campers are welcome to bring their own snack that does not require refrigeration. Please bring a water bottle with your child's name on it, there will be water available to refill their bottles. If your child will be attending both the morning and the afternoon camp sessions, please send them with a packed lunch. All participants for summer camp are encouraged to bring sunscreen each day. We will offer water play days once or twice a week. Campers are asked to bring a towel on water play days and may bring/wear a swimsuit. You will receive an email prior to your camp enrollment with important information, including which days we will have water play.
