Ton 618 -The Largest Known Black Hole

TON 618 is a quasar with a black hole that is believed to be the active galactic nucleus at the center of a galaxy. The mass of the central black hole of TON 618 is at least 66 billion solar masses. This is considered one of the highest masses ever recorded for such an object; higher than the mass of all stars in the Milky Way galaxy combined, which is 64 billion solar masses, and 15,300 times more massive than Sagittarius A*, the Milky Way's central black hole.
